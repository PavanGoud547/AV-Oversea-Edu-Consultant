Study Abroad with AV Overseas Edu

As per government estimates, a little over 9,00,000 Indians were studying abroad in 2022. This grew to 1.31 million in 2023 and 1.33 million in 2024. According to projections, around 1.5 to 2 million Indians will be studying abroad by 2025.

India currently holds the position of the second-largest source of international students, surpassed only by China. In 2019, Indian students spent USD 37 billion on higher education abroad, and this number is expected to soar past USD 70 billion by 2025.

With an investment of INR 30–40 lakhs on a degree Overseass, the returns on that investment — both in career and finances — matter more than ever. Hence, students are increasingly seeking career-ready programs that deliver long-term value.
